Cappuccino with Banana and Sesame seeds

Cappuccinos originate in Italy, and people considered any combination of black coffee and milk a cappuccino. Eventually, frothing became a standard part of the process.

Rating

4.2 (13 votes)

Preparation Time

20m

𝕎𝕒𝕪 𝕆𝕗 ℙ𝕣𝕖𝕡𝕒𝕣𝕚𝕟𝕘

Step 1

Prepare some milk froth

Step 2

Mix the syrup with the froth and stir gently

Step 3

Pour the froth in the cup

Step 4

Pour an espresso (40ml)

Step 5

Place some banana dried chips on the top and sprinkle some sesame seeds as decoration.

ℝ𝕖𝕢𝕦𝕚𝕣𝕖𝕕 𝕀𝕟𝕘𝕣𝕖𝕕𝕚𝕖𝕟𝕥𝕤

1 - Banana Syrup 10ml

2 - Milk 50ml

3 - Dried Banana Chips 5 Unit

4 - Sesame Seeds 1 Teaspoon

5 - Arabica Ethiopia Harrar Capsule 1 Unit
