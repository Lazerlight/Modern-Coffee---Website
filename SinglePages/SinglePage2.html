Step 1

Add water, piloncillo, cinnamon sticks, cloves, and star anise to an olla de barro or medium pot

Step 2

Place over med-high heat and bring to a boil, stirring occasionally so that the piloncillo fully dissolves

Step 3

Remove the olla or pot from the heat and add the coffee. Give it a stir, cover, and let it steep for 6-8 minutes

Step 4

Pour the coffee through a fine mesh strainer or cheesecloth and serve
