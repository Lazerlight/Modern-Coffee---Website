Iced Americano

A classic Americano is an espresso drink made with hot water and espresso, also called a Caffe Americano. The drink can be made with either one or two shots of espresso and varying ratios of water.

Rating

5.0 (3 votes)

Preparation Time

6m

Step 1

Make one doubleshot espresso while using either: espresso machine, portable espresso maker, French press, or Moka pot

Step 2

Pour the espresso into a glass with 1/2 cup ice. Swirl and taste

Step 3

Add cold water, if desired. If you'd like and enjoy
